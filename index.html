About me

My name is Carlos Molina and I am a Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for designing beautiful and functional user experience.

As a Front and Back End Developer, I focus on writing clean, elegant and efficient code.

If I am not in front of my computer working on some code, you will probably find me at the bowling alley or at some random wine/spirits store looking for a nice imported bottle of wine.

I like the outdoors, love to hike and enjoy the challenge of going the extra mile every now and then.

One of my favorite hobbies is photography. I guess is because photographs allow us to share and to communicate with others, it's like a complex language where sometimes a single image will express our feelings for which we cannot find any words.
